Option 01
Midnight Royale
The wedding nobody else has the courage to do
Decor
Midnight tablecloths — never seen in Italy
Tall gold candelabras, hundreds of candles
White florals on dark — dramatic contrast
Your outfits
Jerry: midnight tuxedo with gold lapels
Love: ivory structured gown, long
Gold gele for Love — striking
Why it works: Midnight/black is Yoruba royal. Gold is Nigeria. In Italy nobody does dark tables. Photos will look like a magazine cover.
Option 02
Oro di Lagos
Pure Nigerian joy — the richest, most celebratory palette
Decor
Ivory tablecloths, aso-oke gold runner
Woven baskets, gold, protea centrepieces
Brass lanterns — warm African glow
Your outfits
Love: pure white gown, long veil
Gold embroidery details on gown
Jerry: ebony tuxedo, gold pocket square
Why it works: The most celebratory of all — gold dominates as in the great Lagos/Abuja parties. Perfect for SeeBay or Pagus.
Option 03
Smeraldo Imperiale
The most elegant 2027 palette — Jerry's favourite
Decor
Emerald velvet chairs — deeply opulent
Gold everywhere: candles, cutlery, frames
White roses + deep emerald foliage
Your outfits
Love: white gown with emerald & gold sash
Jerry: black tuxedo, emerald tie
Emerald & gold jewels for Love
Why it works: Emerald is the Igbo colour — Love's heritage without saying it. Timeless, photogenic, extraordinary.
Option 04
Champagne & Borgogna
Silent luxury — the palette of true connoisseurs
Decor
Champagne linen — texture, not flat colour
Burgundy peonies as the floral statement
Ivory & gold candles — absolute warmth
Your outfits
Love: champagne gown (not pure white)
Dramatic bouquet: burgundy & cream
Jerry: black tux, burgundy pocket square
Why it works: The quiet luxury palette. Makes people say "what class" not "what colours." Photographs extraordinarily by candlelight.
Option 05
Blu Adriatico
Born from the sea — perfect if you choose SeeBay
Decor
Navy tablecloths, gold cutlery — marine elegance
Pure white florals — anemones, calla lilies
Blown glass aquamarine centrepieces
Your outfits
Love: white gown, long veil in the breeze
Jerry: navy tuxedo — not the usual black
Nigerian copper details — tie bar
Why it works: This palette was written for SeeBay — Portonovo sea, white cliffs, Adriatic sunset. If you choose SeeBay, this palette chooses itself.
