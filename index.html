Option 01
Midnight Royale
The wedding nobody else has the courage to do
Decor & venue
Midnight tablecloths with gold — never seen in Italy
Tall gold candelabras, hundreds of candles
White & cream florals — dramatic contrast
Dark foliage, olive, pine — Italian depth
Your outfits
Jerry: midnight tuxedo with gold lapels
Love: ivory structured gown, long
Gold accessories — gold gele for Love
Bordeaux & white bouquet — theatrical
Why it works: Midnight/black is Yoruba royal. Gold is Nigeria. In Italy it is revolutionary — nobody does dark tables. The photos will look like a magazine cover. Note: guests need to understand the vision — communicate clearly on the invitation.

Pure Nigerian joy — the richest, most celebratory palette
Decor & venue
Ivory tablecloths, aso-oke gold runner
Woven baskets, gold, protea centrepieces
Brass lanterns — warm African glow
Banana leaves, palms, exotic green
Your outfits
Love: pure white gown, long veil
Gold embroidery details on gown
Jerry: ebony tuxedo, gold pocket square
Copper & gold gele for Love — statement
Why it works: The most celebratory of all five — gold dominates as in the great Lagos/Abuja parties. Works perfectly with Italian July light. Italian guests will feel they are somewhere magical. Perfect for: SeeBay or Pagus.
